FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT THE CCD APP AND HOW TO USE THE EXISTING BEANS FROM THE CCD APP FROM HEREON

Why is the CCD App getting disabled?

We need to improve the functionalities of the app and make it more exciting for our users, therefore the app is going to be removed temporarily.

When is the CCD App going to be back?

CCD App will be back soon with all new features. Timeline for the relaunch will be communicated soon.

Will we be able to order online from CCD without the app?

Yes, you can order online through our new contactless platform - www.ccdonline.in and have a seamless experience.

Can we order takeaway from ccdonline.in as we used to using PICKUP & GO feature on the CCD app?

Yes, you can order takeaway using the PICKUP & GO option through www.ccdonline.in and even schedule your pickups for a particular time of the day.

What will happen to the Beans I earned through the CCD app?

Your Beans are safe and will be transferred to your ccdonline.in account if you register using the same mobile number as used for the app.

I could not use my beans during the lockdown period. Would they have expired?

Considering the Covid-19 lockdown, we have already extended the expiry date of all existing Beans till 31st December 2020. All your Beans are safe and valid till then. You can also choose to use them on www.ccdonline.in.

What will be the validity of the Beans now?

You can use your existing Beans from the app till 31st December 2020.

You just need to log in to the ccdonline.in portal using the same mobile number as that used on the app. Then follow the below mentioned steps -

Users With Existing Beans: (FOR 1st time ccdonline users)

  • Visit www.ccdonline.in
  • Select café or enter the seat code mentioned on the tables
  • Select products from the menu and add them to your cart
  • Proceed to pay, enter the phone number as used on the app and you will receive an OTP
  • On verifying the phone number you will get to see your App Beans on the homepage next to the profile icon on the top right corner (only if same number was provided in the app)
  • You will get to see your balance Beans and will also be allowed to use them at the checkout page based on the applicable terms & conditions

Users With Existing Beans: (FOR existing ccdonline users)

  • Visit www.ccdonline.in
  • Select café or enter the seat code mentioned on the tables
  • Once you are logged in you will get to see your App Beans on the homepage next to the profile icon on the top right corner (only if same number was provided in the app)
  • You will get to see your balance Beans and will also be allowed to use them at the checkout page based on the applicable terms & conditions.
